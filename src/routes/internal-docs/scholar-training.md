---
title: Training Scholars in Anthropology and Sociology
---

Modern fault lines in Hindu society stem from an identity crisis manufactured by the British, by institutionalizing identity-based fault lines through academia and other institutions.

A key strategy was to alter the educational infrastructure and redefine the taxonomy of subjects to perpetually propagate divisions within Hindu society.

This is how disciplines such as sociology and anthropology were structured, but is time to rethink these disciplines.

At Bodha, we address this by training scholars every year in anthropology and sociology from an Indic point of view.
