<p><a href="/library/art-experience" class="blank linked">Art Experience</a>, by M Hiriyanna - Reflection on art, meaning, symbolism, and the experience of beauty in Indian thought.</p>
<p><a href="/library/hindustani-music" class="blank linked">Hindustani Music</a>, by Lakshmi Sreeram - Overview of Hindustani music theory, form, pedagogy, and performance practice.</p>
<p><a href="/library/indian-concept-of-performance" class="blank linked">Indian Concept of Performance</a>, by Bharat Gupt - Study of performance as a conceptual and embodied category in Indian aesthetic theory.</p>
<p><a href="/library/on-natyashastra" class="blank linked">On Natyashastra</a>, by Bharat Gupt - Introductory or interpretive study of the Natyashastra and its framework for drama, performance, and rasa.</p>
<p><a href="/library/the-kalas" class="blank linked">The Kalās</a>, by A Venkatasubbiah - Work on Indian Knowledge Systems, bringing traditional categories into conversation with practice, theory, or pedagogy.</p>
