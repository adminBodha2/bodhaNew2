---
title: History, Polity, and Society
description: Works on political thought, governance, social structure, chronicles, institutions, and historical memory in Indian civilization.
---

<p><a href="/library/arthashastra" class="blank linked">Arthashastra</a>, by Kautilya - Classical treatise on statecraft, administration, diplomacy, economy, espionage, and royal governance.</p>
<p><a href="/library/civil-disobedience-in-indian-tradition" class="blank linked">Civil Disobedience in Indian Tradition</a>, by Dharampal - Study of resistance, legitimacy, and non-cooperation within Indian political and ethical traditions.</p>
<p><a href="/library/gandhis-theory-of-society-and-our-times" class="blank linked">Gandhis Theory of Society and Our Times</a>, by AK Saran - Interpretive study of Gandhi’s social philosophy, moral politics, and continuing relevance.</p>
<p><a href="/library/panchayat-raj-and-indias-polity" class="blank linked">Panchayat Raj and India's Polity</a>, by Dharampal - Study of village governance and decentralized political structures in Indian constitutional and civilizational context.</p>
<p><a href="/library/rajatarangini" class="blank linked">Rajatarangini</a>, by Ranjit Sitaram Pandit - Chronicle of Kashmir that blends political history, dynastic memory, and literary historiography.</p>
<p><a href="/library/rediscovering-india" class="blank linked">Rediscovering India</a>, by Dharampal - Essays aimed at recovering Indian civilizational categories, intellectual memory, and cultural self-knowledge.</p>
