---
title: Dharma, Ethics, and Law
description: Texts dealing with duty, moral order, legal reasoning, social norms, ritual obligations, and the dharmashastra tradition.
---

<p><a href="/library/history-of-dharmashastras-vol-1" class="blank linked">History of Dharmashastras Vol 1</a>, by PV Kane - Reference study of dharmashastra literature, sources of dharma, and the historical formation of legal-ritual norms.</p>
<p><a href="/library/history-of-dharmashastras-vol-2-part-1" class="blank linked">History of Dharmashastras Vol 2 Part 1</a>, by PV Kane - Reference study of dharmashastra literature with focus on ritual, samskara, and normative practice.</p>
<p><a href="/library/history-of-dharmashastras-vol-2-part-2" class="blank linked">History of Dharmashastras Vol 2 Part 2</a>, by PV Kane - Reference study of dharmashastra literature continuing discussion of rites, duties, and normative institutions.</p>
<p><a href="/library/history-of-dharmashastras-vol-3" class="blank linked">History of Dharmashastras Vol 3</a>, by PV Kane - Reference study of dharmashastra themes including social duties, legal procedure, and normative ordering.</p>
<p><a href="/library/history-of-dharmashastras-vol-4" class="blank linked">History of Dharmashastras Vol 4</a>, by PV Kane - Reference study of dharmashastra themes with detailed treatment of law, custom, and social institutions.</p>
<p><a href="/library/history-of-dharmashastras-vol-5-part-1" class="blank linked">History of Dharmashastras Vol 5 Part 1</a>, by PV Kane - Reference study of later dharmashastra topics, jurisprudence, and social-religious regulation.</p>
<p><a href="/library/history-of-dharmashastras-vol-5-part-2" class="blank linked">History of Dharmashastras Vol 5 Part 2</a>, by PV Kane - Reference study completing the dharmashastra series with later legal and ritual materials.</p>
<p><a href="/library/hindu-dharma" class="blank linked">Hindu Dharma</a>, by S. Radhakrishnan - General account of Hindu dharma, its philosophical foundations, social vision, and practical disciplines.</p>
<p><a href="/library/elementary-textbook-hindu-religion-ethics" class="blank linked">Elementary Textbook of Hindu Religion and Ethics</a>, by  - Introductory textbook on Hindu beliefs, duties, virtues, and everyday ethical formation.</p>
<p><a href="/library/advanced-textbook-hindu-religion-ethics" class="blank linked">Advanced Textbook of Hindu Religion and Ethics</a>, by  - More advanced textbook on Hindu ethics, doctrine, discipline, and moral reasoning.</p>
<p><a href="/library/hindu-dharma-radhakrishnan" class="blank linked">Hindu Dharma Radhakrishnan</a>, by S. Radhakrishnan - Essay by S. Radhakrishnan presenting Hindu dharma as a living, adaptive, and philosophically grounded religious tradition.</p>
