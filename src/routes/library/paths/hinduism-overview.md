---
title: Hinduism Overview
description: Broad introductory works that summarize Hindu tradition as a whole, including its beliefs, practices, worldview, and civilizational continuity.
---

<p><a href="/library/essential-coomaraswamy" class="blank linked">Essential Coomaraswamy</a>, by Ananda Coomaraswamy - General overview of Hindu tradition, covering its beliefs, practices, social vision, and intellectual foundations.</p>
<p><a href="/library/future-of-mankind" class="blank linked">Future of Mankind</a>, by Sri Aurobindo - Civilizational reflection arguing for a spiritually grounded future rooted in perennial wisdom.</p>
<p><a href="/library/the-beautiful-tree" class="blank linked">The Beautiful Tree</a>, by Dharampal - Historical argument about indigenous education in India before colonial disruption.</p>
<p><a href="/library/religious-sects-of-hindus" class="blank linked">Religious Sects of Hindus</a>, by HH Wilson - Survey of major Hindu sects, their doctrines, institutions, and devotional lineages.</p>
<p><a href="/library/all-about-hindus" class="blank linked">All About Hinduism</a>, by Sri Swami Sivananda - Popular overview of Hindu beliefs, practices, festivals, symbols, and philosophical foundations.</p>
<p><a href="/library/all-about-hinduism" class="blank linked">Hinduism Ancient and Modern</a>, by Rai Bahadur Lala Baij Nath - Comparative overview of continuities and changes in Hindu tradition across historical periods.</p>
