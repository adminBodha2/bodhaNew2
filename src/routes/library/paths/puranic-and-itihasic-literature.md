---
title: Puranic and Itihasic Literature
description: Narrative and sacred-historical texts such as the Puranas, Harivamsha, epic-related studies, and story literature preserving cosmology, myth, genealogy, and sacred geography.
---

<p><a href="/library/agni-purana" class="blank linked">Agni Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Agni Purana, an encyclopedic Purana covering cosmology, ritual, iconography, kingship, and sacred practice.</p>
<p><a href="/library/bhagavata-purana" class="blank linked">Bhagavata Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Bhagavata Purana, a major Vaishnava Purana centered on Krishna, bhakti, cosmology, and liberation.</p>
<p><a href="/library/brahma-purana" class="blank linked">Brahma Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Brahma Purana, creation narratives, sacred geography, pilgrimage, ritual life, and dynastic lore.</p>
<p><a href="/library/brahmanda-purana" class="blank linked">Brahmanda Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Brahmanda Purana, cosmology, cycles of time, genealogies, and later devotional theology.</p>
<p><a href="/library/garuda-purana" class="blank linked">Garuda Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Garuda Purana, afterlife teachings, funerary rites, ethics, cosmology, and Vaishnava doctrine.</p>
<p><a href="/library/geography-of-the-puranas" class="blank linked">Geography of the Puranas</a>, by MA Ali - Edition or translation of the Geography of the Puranas, a Purana text combining mythology, cosmology, ritual observance, and sacred geography.</p>
<p><a href="/library/harivansha-purana" class="blank linked">Harivansha Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Harivansha Purana, Krishna lineage narratives and cosmological material closely tied to the Mahabharata tradition.</p>
<p><a href="/library/kurma-purana" class="blank linked">Kurma Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Kurma Purana, cosmology, pilgrimage, dharma, and sectarian theology in dialogic form.</p>
<p><a href="/library/linga-purana" class="blank linked">Linga Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Linga Purana, Shaiva theology of the linga alongside cosmology, vows, and sacred geography.</p>
<p><a href="/library/markandeya-purana" class="blank linked">Markandeya Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Markandeya Purana, mythic and ethical narrative best known for the Devi Mahatmya and broader cosmological material.</p>
<p><a href="/library/matsya-purana" class="blank linked">Matsya Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Matsya Purana, the flood myth together with temple, iconographic, genealogical, and pilgrimage material.</p>
<p><a href="/library/narada-purana" class="blank linked">Narada Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Narada Purana, ritual, devotion, pilgrimage, and religious observance in a Purana compendium format.</p>
<p><a href="/library/padma-purana" class="blank linked">Padma Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Padma Purana, creation, pilgrimage, dharma, sacred places, and large Vaishnava devotional sections.</p>
<p><a href="/library/shiva-purana" class="blank linked">Shiva Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Shiva Purana, myths, worship, cosmology, and theology centered on Shiva.</p>
<p><a href="/library/skanda-purana" class="blank linked">Skanda Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Skanda Purana, a vast pilgrimage-oriented Purana organized around sacred geography and regional mahatmyas.</p>
<p><a href="/library/vamana-purana" class="blank linked">Vamana Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Vamana Purana, mythic narrative, pilgrimage, and Vaishnava themes around the Vamana tradition.</p>
<p><a href="/library/varaha-purana" class="blank linked">Varaha Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Varaha Purana, Vaishnava cosmology, ritual observance, pilgrimage, and the Varaha incarnation.</p>
<p><a href="/library/vayu-purana" class="blank linked">Vayu Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Vayu Purana, cosmology, genealogies, dynastic memory, and ancient sacred lore.</p>
<p><a href="/library/vishnu-purana" class="blank linked">Vishnu Purana</a>, by  - Edition or translation of the Vishnu Purana, creation, dynasties, avatara doctrine, cosmology, and dharma in a foundational Vaishnava Purana.</p>
<p><a href="/library/argument-and-design-the-unity-of-the-mahabharata" class="blank linked">Argument and Design the Unity of the Mahabharata</a>, by Vishwa Adluri, Joydeep Bagchee - Study arguing for the literary and philosophical unity of the Mahabharata against fragmenting critical models.</p>
<p><a href="/library/katha-sarit-sagar" class="blank linked">Katha Sarit Sagar</a>, by C.H. Tawney - Large compendium of narrative literature preserving story cycles, moral reflection, and courtly imagination.</p>
<p><a href="/library/prolegomena-to-any-future-mahabharata-studies" class="blank linked">Prolegomena to any Future Mahabharata Studies</a>, by Vishwa Adluri, Joydeep Bagchee - Methodological essay rethinking how the Mahabharata should be studied, edited, and interpreted.</p>
